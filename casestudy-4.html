Maverick Systems: Transforming Real Estate Insights Through Cloud Strategy

Ataviz Consulting partnered with Maverick Systems, a leading provider of solutions for real estate brokerages, to modernize their data infrastructure and unlock actionable insights across multiple markets. The engagement focused on enabling scalable, data-driven decision-making for agent recruitment, coaching, and market strategy.

Challenge

Maverick Systems faced fragmented MLS data sources that limited reporting speed, data accessibility, and analytical capability. The company needed a centralized, scalable environment to manage large volumes of agent and brokerage data securely while supporting advanced analytics and real-time reporting.

Strategy

Ataviz Consulting designed and executed a comprehensive cloud migration and data modernization plan:

  • Developed a cloud-based data warehouse optimized for real estate analytics.
  • Consolidated multiple MLS data sources into structured, query-ready datasets for agents and brokerages.
  • Implemented automated data pipelines to ensure real-time syncing and monthly reporting.
  • Established cloud-native data management practices emphasizing security, performance, and scalability.
  • Provided ongoing guidance on managing and expanding the cloud infrastructure to support evolving business needs.

Outcome

The centralized cloud infrastructure enabled Maverick Systems to:

  • Deliver faster, more reliable monthly reports and real-time data access across regions.
  • Reduce infrastructure maintenance and increase system resilience.
  • Streamline onboarding of new MLS sources and analytic features as business needs evolve.
  • Position the company as a data-first leader in real estate, empowering brokerages with actionable insights for agent recruitment and competitive strategy.
