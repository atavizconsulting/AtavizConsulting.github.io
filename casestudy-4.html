Revolutionizing Real Estate Insights for Maverick Systems Through Cloud-Based Custom Analytics

In a transformative partnership, Maverick Systems engaged Ataviz Consulting to lead a comprehensive cloud migration and data modernization initiative that would revolutionize how data is stored, accessed, and leveraged in the real estate industry. By migrating fragmented MLS data sources to a centralized, cloud-based data warehouse, Ataviz Consulting enabled Maverick Systems to deliver faster, more scalable insights for real estate brokerages focused on agent recruitment, coaching, and market strategy.

Goal:

Maverick Systems, a leading provider of integrated solutions for real estate brokerages, set out to modernize its data infrastructure to support advanced analytics and enhance service delivery. The primary objective was to centralize and optimize access to large-scale agent and brokerage data by transitioning to a cloud-native environment. With Ataviz Consulting at the helm, the project focused on securely migrating, organizing, and managing high-volume MLS data across diverse markets—laying the groundwork for dynamic, insight-driven decision-making.

Tools:

Ataviz Consulting orchestrated the full cloud migration lifecycle—from strategic planning and architecture design to implementation and post-migration management. The project involved:

  • Extraction and transformation of over 1,000,000 records and 500+ data columns from multiple MLS systems via API integration.
  • Design and deployment of a scalable cloud data warehouse tailored for real estate analytics.
  • Creation of two structured datasets—one for real estate agents and one for brokerages—optimized for querying, reporting, and analysis.
  • Implementation of cloud-native data management practices, ensuring security, performance, and scalability.
  • Automation of data pipelines to ensure real-time syncing and monthly reporting capabilities.

Results:

By migrating its data infrastructure to the cloud, Maverick Systems unlocked new levels of speed, scalability, and insight generation. The centralized cloud warehouse—built and managed by Ataviz Consulting—serves as the foundation for all data-driven initiatives, enabling:

  • Faster and more reliable monthly reporting.
  • Real-time access to up-to-date data across regions.
  • Reduced infrastructure maintenance and improved system resilience.
  • Streamlined onboarding of new MLS sources or custom features as business needs evolve.

The result? Maverick Systems is now positioned as a data-first leader in real estate solutions—empowering clients with intelligent tools for agent recruitment, coaching, and competitive intelligence. The success of this cloud migration showcases Ataviz Consulting’s ability to lead complex, high-impact digital transformations in data-centric industries.
