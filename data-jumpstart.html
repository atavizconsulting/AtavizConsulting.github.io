Data Jumpstart

Overview

Ataviz Consulting understands the challenges that small businesses face when it comes to implementing data analytics. That's why we created our Data Jumpstart service, specifically designed to help small businesses get started with data analytics quickly and easily.

Key Features

Google Analytics Setup: We will set up and configure Google Analytics for your business, including tracking website traffic, user behavior, and other key performance indicators (KPIs) that are important to your business.

Google Ads Management: We will create and manage Google Ads and run one full campaign for your business, including keyword research, ad copywriting, targeting, and tracking conversions. We will also help optimize the campaigns to maximize ROI.

Quickbooks Integration: We will integrate Quickbooks with other data sources, such as Google Analytics, to create a more comprehensive view of your business. This can include tracking revenue and expenses, monitoring cash flow, and generating financial reports.

Data Strategy Consulting: We will provide guidance on developing a data strategy for your business, including identifying key performance indicators (KPIs) and setting up processes for data collection, storage, and analysis.

Training and Support: We will provide training and support to help you and your team learn how to use the data analytics tools and services effectively. This can include one-on-one coaching, online tutorials, and user guides.

Data Visualization: We will help you create one customized, interactive dashboard using Google Data Studio. This powerful tool provides insights tailored to your business needs, such as tracking website traffic, sales, and marketing campaign performance.

With our Data Jumpstart service, you can take advantage of the power of data analytics to grow your business, without the need for a large in-house team or expertise. Contact us today to learn more about how Ataviz Consulting can help you get started with data analytics.

Expected Outcomes

  • Gain access to important data insights to make better-informed decisions about marketing strategies, finances, and overall business operations
  • Set up and manage data infrastructures
  • Track key performance indicators
  • Visualize data in ways that are easy to understand and act upon
  • Identify opportunities for growth and optimization of marketing campaigns
  • Improve the bottom line
  • Empower small business owners and their teams with the skills and knowledge to continue using data analytics effectively over the long term
