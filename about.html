About Us

Our Story

Welcome to Ataviz Consulting, a data analytics consulting company dedicated to providing businesses of all sizes with access to the power of data analytics. We believe that every business can benefit from data analytics, and we are here to ensure that all businesses, regardless of their size, have the expertise and technology they need to thrive.

Ataviz Consulting was founded by Nicholas Johnson, our lead data scientist. He brings extensive experience in data analysis and data science, and combines it with a background in math education and sales. Our mission is to empower businesses by harnessing the potential of data analytics, enabling them to make data-driven decisions that drive growth, profitability, and customer satisfaction.

We firmly believe that data analytics is vital to the success of any business. That's why we are committed to providing businesses of all sizes with the necessary support and expertise to make informed decisions based on data.

Our Approach

At Ataviz Consulting, we specialize in helping businesses of all sizes make data-driven decisions. With a proven track record across various industries, we pride ourselves on delivering tailored solutions that meet each client's unique needs.

Our comprehensive services encompass data visualization, predictive analytics, and business intelligence. By working closely with our clients, we gain a deep understanding of their goals and objectives, leveraging our expertise to provide actionable insights that drive informed decision-making.

Our approach is rooted in collaboration and transparency. We value close partnerships with our clients, ensuring they have a clear grasp of their data and its potential for driving growth. By fostering collaboration, we empower our clients to make the best decisions for their businesses.

Our Values

At Ataviz Consulting, transparency and accountability are at the core of our values. We are committed to providing our clients with clear, concise reporting that is both understandable and actionable. Our goal is to ensure that our clients always have a clear understanding of their data and how it can be utilized to fuel success and growth.

We are dedicated to helping businesses thrive, regardless of their size. By providing businesses of all sizes with the same level of expertise and technology as larger corporations, we contribute to building stronger communities and a robust economy.

Thank you for considering Ataviz Consulting for your data analytics needs. We are excited about the opportunity to help your business succeed. If you have any questions or would like to learn more about our services, please do not hesitate to contact us.
