About Us

Our Story

Welcome to Ataviz Consulting, where our mission is to bring the power of data analytics for businesses of all sizes. Established in March 2020, Ataviz Consulting embarked on a journey to make the transformative potential of data analytics accessible to every business, irrespective of its size. In a landscape dominated by tech giants like Google, Amazon, and Facebook, we believe that every business deserves access to the insights and advantages that data analytics can offer. Our commitment is rooted in the belief that every business deserves access to the transformative potential of data analytics, regardless of its size.

Our Founder

Ataviz Consulting draws its strength from the vision and expertise of its founder, Nicholas Johnson. With over 15 years of unparalleled experience in statistical analysis, building data dashboards, and automation, Nick has not only left an indelible mark as a distinguished data analyst and Tableau developer but has also become a guiding force in the realm of data science. Nick's journey is a testament to his commitment to excellence, working across various industries and providing valuable insights, including his role as a government consultant.

Beyond his technical acumen, Nick brings a unique blend of education and sales experience to our team. Holding a degree in mathematics with a focus on statistical analysis, as well as a master's degree in education, Nick's multidisciplinary background enriches the Ataviz Consulting approach. His passion for combining mathematical precision with the practical applications of data analytics has been instrumental in shaping our mission to empower businesses through the transformative potential of data.

Our Mission

At Ataviz Consulting, our mission is to make the benefits of data analytics accessible to all businesses. We understand that embracing data analytics can feel challenging for many, regardless of their scale. However, we are here to simplify the process and showcase how tapping into the power of data can drive success, profitability, and sustainability. We envision a future where every business, regardless of its unique characteristics, can thrive through informed, data-driven decision-making.

Our Approach

We are committed to guiding businesses towards the transformative potential of data-driven decision-making. Our approach is designed to cater to the unique needs and goals of each client. Specializing in crafting tailored solutions encompassing data visualization, predictive analytics, and business intelligence, we prioritize close collaboration with our clients. Our commitment extends beyond delivering solutions; we aim to empower our clients by helping them comprehend the full potential of their data in driving growth and success. In navigating the vast landscape of data analytics, we prioritize transparency, ensuring our clients are well-equipped to make informed decisions.

Our Values

At Ataviz Consulting, transparency and accountability are fundamental to our values. We are committed to delivering clear, concise reporting that is both understandable and actionable. Our goal is to ensure that our clients always have a clear understanding of their data and its potential for fueling success and growth. We are dedicated to leveling the playing field for businesses of all sizes, we provide the same level of expertise and technology as larger corporations, contributing to building stronger communities and a robust economy. Our values extend beyond technical solutions, emphasizing our commitment to ethical and responsible data practices.

Thank you for considering Ataviz Consulting for your data analytics needs. We look forward to the opportunity to help your business thrive. If you have any questions or would like to learn more about our services, please do not hesitate to contact us.
