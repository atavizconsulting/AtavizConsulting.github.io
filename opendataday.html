Visualizing Public Art Data with Tableau - Presentation Materials

Thank you to all the participants who attended Nick Johnson's presentation, "Visualizing Syracuse Public Art Data with Tableau," at Syracuse Open Data Day 2023. Your presence and engagement made this event a great success.
